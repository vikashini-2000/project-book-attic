DELPHI'S CHOOSEN

5.0 rating

By A.K.PATCH

cost : 450/-

About The Book

The seven demigods needed to stop Gaea and her Giant children from destroying the Olympians in Greece to destroy the world, could be a reference to the seven continents around the world. This is the second prophecy which ended with the lines '…breath' and '… death' the first being the prophecy for the quest of the labyrinth. This is the first prophecy to have its full meaning decoded before its events came to pass. It was figured out by Jason, Piper and Leo in the House of Hades, while the events occurred later. According to Hera, the seven demigods of Prophecy will be the greatest heroes of all time.
